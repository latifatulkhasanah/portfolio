Approach

Strategy before spend.

Every campaign I run starts with brand strategy, not budget. I build out core values and positioning, define the emotional and functional benefit for the audience, and back it with a clear reason to believe — before a single rupiah goes into media.

That foundation feeds into a communication strategy split across three campaign types, each calibrated to a different stage of the funnel:

  • Always-on Campaign — continuous, always-running activity that sustains reach and retargeting
  • Thematic Campaign — seasonal or brand-moment pushes tied to a bigger narrative
  • Tactical Campaign — short, targeted bursts built around a specific conversion goal
